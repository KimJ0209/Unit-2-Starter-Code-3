Section 3: The life of the idols

In order to be an Idol, you would have to go through training to be ready for debut, sounds kinda easy right? It wasn't really easy. The idols have to go through a lot of expectations since South Korea is known for it's Beauty Standard. In the Us the Beauty Standard is to be slim, be blonde/brunette, blue eyes etc but in Korea it's impossible to fit into the Beauty Standard, small v-shaped face, fair skin, symmetrical eyebrows, a slim body, and double eyelids and so much more now imagine what idols have to go through.
